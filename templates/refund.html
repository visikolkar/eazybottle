Cancellation & Refund Policy

The online order once placed can be modified or cancelled through the mobile App. In case the order is paid through Online Banking and if cancelled from Eazybottle, due to non-availability of the ordered product, the amount will be returned by reversing the transaction by Eazybottle. The same would reflect in the customer account within 7 working days approximately.

In-transit orders as well as scheduled orders can be cancelled. There are two types
In case of wallet
  • You can cancel the ongoing order and after cancellation the can(s) will be reflected back to your cart as remaining balance. Or
In case of payment via Online Banking
  • You can cancel the ongoing and scheduled deliveries by visiting Deliveries under Orders from the app and select cancel order, the same shall initiate the refund for cancelled can(s).

Refunds

  • Deposit Eazybottle shall process the refund request of deposit amount paid during sign up.
  • Remaining Can(s) in the Wallet
The refunds are solely based upon the number of cans purchased by you. Eazybottle will not refund for any of the offers that are made by it and the bottles that you have

For the Bottle purchased through Exchange offer, upon Refund request you need to return the same new Bottle you got from the EazyBottle during the sign up to process your request.
Eazybottle keeps a track of the free bottles it offered and the same is not entitled for refunds.
